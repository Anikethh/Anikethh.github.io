I currently work as a research fellow at TCS Research, India, where I'm fortunate to be advised by Manasi Patwardhan and Lovekesh Vig. Previously, I graduated from IIIT Delhi in 2024 with B.Tech (Honors) in Computer Science and Design. I briefly interned at MathWorks in 2023 working on symbolic code generation using LLMs.

My efforts are focused towards AI4Research, where I explore how AI can complement researchers in the scientific research cycle through brainstorming, ideation, retrieval and experimentation. More broadly, I am interested in AI for Scientific Discovery.
