About

I work as a research fellow at TCS Research, India, where fortunate to be I'm advised by Manasi Patwardhan and Lovekesh Vig. Previously, I graduated from IIIT Delhi in 2024 with B.Tech (w/ Honors) in Computer Science and Design. I briefly interned at MathWorks in 2023 working on symbolic code generation using LLMs

My current efforts are focused on AI4Research, where I explore how AI can complement researchers in the scientific research cycle through brainstorming, ideation, retrieval and experimentation. More broadly, I am interested in AI for Scientific Discovery and Language Model Interpretability.
